Quinoa & Chickpea Power Bowl
15 min
2 servings
350 cal
✓ All ingredients are safe based on your profile
Ingredients
•
1 cup pre-cooked quinoa (or use microwave packet)
•
1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
•
2 cups mixed greens (spinach, kale, arugula)
•
1 cucumber, diced
•
1 tomato, diced
•
2 tbsp olive oil
•
1 tbsp lemon juice
•
Salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs
Instructions
1.
Heat chickpeas in a pan with a drizzle of olive oil for 5 minutes until slightly crispy.
2.
If using uncooked quinoa, microwave according to package directions (usually 90 seconds).
3.
In a bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, and tomato.
4.
Add warm quinoa and chickpeas on top.
5.
Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Enjoy!
